About Us

Pace institute of technology and sciences

PACE Institute of Technology and Sciences (PACE ITS) is an engineering college established in 2007. It offers B.Tech, M.Tech and MBA programs. The college is committed to creating a world-class technical education. PACE ITS has a placement cell that helps students get placed in reputed companies.

Some of the companies that recruited students from PACE ITS include Accenture, Deloitte, Yexle, Lanarsy, Movate, Raster Engineers, Gestamp, Shriram Finance, Bajaj Fincorp, Moldtek, and Ragami Solutions..
